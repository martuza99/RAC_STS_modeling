The variability of Recycled Concrete Aggregates (RCAs) poses a significant challenge in predicting the mechanical properties of Recycled Aggregate Concrete (RAC), particularly its splitting tensile strength (STS). Accurate and interpretable predictive models are essential for supporting mix‑design decisions and promoting the use of sustainable construction materials. This study develops Deep Neural Network (DNN) models to predict the STS of RAC.

The experimental dataset used in this study was obtained from the publicly available supplementary materials that can be accessed directly at: https://static-content.springer.com/esm/art%3A10.1038%2Fs41598-023-36303-0/MediaObjects/41598_2023_36303_MOESM1_ESM.docx
